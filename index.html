The objective of this project is to enable users to create an inexpensive laser projector out of things that you may already have and are easy to obtain if you do not.

To create the laser projector itself you will need the following:

  • Powered computer speakers
  • Laser pointer
  • Small craft mirrors
  • Small diameter dowel rods
  • Craft popsicle sticks
  • Hot glue

Click here for a similar project that will give you an idea how the projector goes together.

Frequency X represents the X (horizontal) axis

Frequency Y represents the Y (vertical) axis

The X axis is output through the left audio channel and the Y axis is output through the right audio channel.

Once your projector is built you can manipulate the frequency and wave form of the two axes to draw shapes with your laser
